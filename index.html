Jerry Xiong

I'm a joint BS-MS student in Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I completed my undergrad in two years, from Fall 2021 to Spring 2023, where I worked with Professor Matthew Gombolay. Since I started my Master's in Fall 2023, I have been working with Professor Dhruv Batra. My research interests are broadly related to advancing generalizable embodied intelligence by exploring novel ways to leverage large-scale datasets, with connections to reinforcement learning and self-supervised learning.
