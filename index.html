Jerry Xiong

Hello. I have graduated from Georgia Tech's BS/MS in CS program. I completed my undergrad in two years, from Fall 2021 to Spring 2023, where I worked with Professor Matthew Gombolay. I completed my Master's in one year, from Fall 2023 to Spring 2024, where I worked with Professor Dhruv Batra. My research interests are broadly related to advancing generalizable embodied intelligence by exploring novel ways to leverage large-scale datasets, with connections to reinforcement learning and self-supervised learning.
